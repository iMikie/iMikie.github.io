Reviewing RubyMine in 5 minutes

As neophytes at DevBootCamp we edit a Debugger statement into the code, save it, go to the terminal, open irb or pry, load the file, run it, hit the debug statement, type out some variables, etc.

If having your hand leave the keyboard for the mouse slows you down, then leaving your entire environment to reload and run your program has gotta be orders of magnitude worse.

I've been checking out RubyMine to see if it will take my Ruby to the next level. I'm really liking it. I've endeavored to show in 5 minutes a few things you can use right away.

Thanks for watching.
